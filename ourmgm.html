OUR MANAGEMENT

As one of Sri Lanka’s foremost Building and Civil Engineering Organizations, our strength is drawn from our Management Team, who is the backbone of our Company. Integrating strong values into our business model, they continue to anticipate and exceed our customers’ requirements, ensuring that we grow in success as the years go by.

Harsha De Saram

Chairman

Mr. De Saram was appointed to the position of Chairman in 2012 and is the Co-founder of ICC. He successfully chairs a multitude of companies within the group, ranging from manufacturing and hospitality to property development. He is the driving force behind the Group’s innovation, ensuring its steady growth over the past 35 years.

M.P.N.S. Peiris

Chief Executive Officer

Taking over as CEO from 2006, Mr. Peiris started his career with ICC as early as 1988. Drawing from his former experience working for multinational construction companies, both in Sri Lanka and Oman, he has continued to contribute greatly to the Company’s success, . A Chartered Civil Engineer, he holds a BSc. (Eng) and C.Eng MIE (SL) from the University of Moratuwa and a MBA from the University of Colombo.

V.S. Nagodavithane

Executive Director - Designs & Estimates

A Chartered Civil Engineer from the University of Ceylon, Mr. Nagodavithane holds a BSc. Eng. (Hons) CEng,MICE (Lon) MIE (SL). With over 40 years of design and construction experience locally and internationally, his expertise propelled him to the post of Director in 1997 after 4 years at the Company.

R.I.M.L.K. Jayasinghe

Executive Director - Roads & Bridges

Mr. Jayasinghe joined ICC in 1993 and was appointed to the role of Director – Roads & Bridges, in 1997. He is a Chartered Civil Engineer from the University of Peradeniya, and holds a BSc. Eng (Hons) CEng, MIE (SL) and two Post Graduate Diplomas in Construction Management & Highway Traffic Engineering, as well as a Diploma in Commercial Arbitration and possesses 15 years of experience in both local and overseas construction projects.

Indika Jayasuriya

Executive Director - Plant & Equipment / International Division

With over 32 years of working in the Group, Mr. Jayasuriya has experience ranging from Engineering Workshop Management to establishing the Readymix, Quarrying and Crushing Plants. He holds a Diploma in Management and was appointed to the role of Director in 1997. He is currently responsible for all Plant & Equipment, Pre-Cast Manufacturing and International Projects.

Chula Thenuwara

Executive Director - Projects

Mr. Thenuwara spearheads the Projects Divison and is a National Diploma holder for Civil Engineering from the University of Moratuwa and a Member of IIESL. He joined ICC at its inception, following 3 years’ experience in the Mahaweli Development Board and was appointed as Director - Projects in 2016.
