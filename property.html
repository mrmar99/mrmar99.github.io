Nivasie Developers

Gaining a strong reputation as a responsible property development company, Nivasie Developers was established in 2000. Following the successful completion of its primary project, ‘Nivasiepura’; a 1202 house gated community in Ja-Ela, the Company has completed several projects over the years including, Hill Crest Estate in Madapatha, Victoria Range Bungalows in Kandy, Orchid Project in Malabe and Orchid Apartments, Nivasie’s first venture into apartments.

Yielding a guaranteed high market rate return on investment, Nivasie homes continue to enjoy a significant increase in demand, delivering high quality completed projects within a relatively short period of time, leading to over 30% repeat clients with each project.

Find out more on http://www.orchidbynivasie.com/

Oceanfront Condominiums

Spacious two and three bedroom apartments with a magnificent garden, swimming pool, fully-equipped gym and on-site café that are on-par with any luxury hotel, Oceanfront Condominiums is set upon the pristine white sandy beaches of Nilaveli, Trincomalee, making it a great investment opportunity.

The first beach-front, serviced, luxury leisure apartment complex in Trincomalee, Oceanfront Condominiums consist of 81 units, ranging from 1180sqft-2100sqft that blend with its natural surroundings and offer a magnificent view of the Indian Ocean.

An ideal second home or holiday getaway, these condominiums yield a high return on investment. What’s more, ICC offers check-in and check-out services, housekeeping and laundry, and acts as a facilitator to rent your furnished unit. Or simply opt for the turn-key option offered by us to furnish, advertise and manage your apartment, making your investment even more attractive and hassle free.

Find out more on http://oceanfrontnilaveli.com/ and http://oceanfrontcondos.lk

Iceland Business Centre

A salute to our commitment to building a sustainable development, Iceland Business Centre is ICC’s first venture into managed commercial real estate. Designed to meet LEEDS Platinum Certification, the building’s features include solar farm, rain water harvesting, raised floors, low-E external glass, improved water conservation, an integrated building management system and a natural green wall which absorbs heat.
